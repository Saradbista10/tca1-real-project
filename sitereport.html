Portfolio Site Report

1. Overview

Sarad's Portfolio is a personal website designed to showcase the work, skills, and experience of Sarad Bista, a passionate web developer. The site serves as an online portfolio, providing visitors with a comprehensive view of Sarad's projects, video demos, and contact information. It aims to highlight his technical abilities and creative solutions in web development..

2. Sections of the Website

  • Home : In home page, There is introduction with a brief welcome message.
  • Video Demo: A section dedicated to video demonstrations of projects and it provides visual and interactive insights into the projects.
  • Project: In project page, A gallery of selected projects with descriptions and links to view them in detail and Projects include various web development tasks demonstrating different skills and technologies.
  • Contact: A form for visitors to reach out to Sarad and including email and phone number.
  • Site Report: Detailed documentation of the website's creation process. Overview of the structure, design choices, and technologies used.

3. Technologies Used

  • HTML: For structuring the content.
  • CSS: For styling the website.
  • Responsive Design: The website is fully responsive, ensuring it looks great on all devices, from desktops to smartphones..

4. Detailed Description

The portfolio website includes a Home section with a welcoming message, a Projects section showcasing key projects with descriptions and links, and a Contact section with a form for visitors to get in touch. Each section is designed to be clear and accessible, ensuring a good user experience.
